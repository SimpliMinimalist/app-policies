Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Todoo is a minimalist to-do application designed to keep your productivity data private. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled.

2. Personal Data

The app does not collect, request, or store any personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location. All features can be used anonymously.

3. Local Storage

All tasks, completion status, and app settings (such as themes) are stored locally on your device.

  • No access to your tasks
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • Data is permanently deleted if the app is uninstalled or cleared

4. Third-Party Services (Payments)

Google Play Billing is used to process premium subscriptions.

  • Payment information is handled directly by Google
  • Only purchase status is received to unlock features
  • Refer to Google Play’s privacy policy for details

5. Data Security

Since data remains on your device, its security depends on your device’s protection. No tracking, analytics, or cookies are used.

6. Changes

This policy may be updated occasionally. Updates will be reflected on this page.
